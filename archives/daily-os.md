# Daily Operating System

*Review this every morning. Should take under 2 minutes.*

---

## The 3 Non-Negotiables (every day, ~30 min total)

These run regardless of how fragmented the day is. If you only do these, the day was not wasted.

| # | What | Time Cap |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Protect Income** - Check + respond to all Fiverr/Upwork messages | 15 min |
| 2 | **Run the Machine** - Verify outreach pipeline ran (cold email, LinkedIn) | 5 min |
| 3 | **Ship One Thing** - One output: proposal, post, follow-up, or system improvement | 15 min |

---

## Priority Filter (for remaining time)

Apply top-down. Never skip a level if the one above has open items.

1. **Active client delivery** - Anything owed to a paying client
2. **Warm lead follow-up** - Anyone who replied or needs a proposal
3. **System building** - Improve acquisition or content infrastructure
4. **Cold acquisition / content** - New outreach, new posts, new leads

---

## Day-Type Map

| Day | Mode | Focus |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Deep Work | Client delivery + system building (9:30am - 2:50pm) |
| Tuesday | Medium | Warm leads + proposals (after 2:45pm) |
| Wednesday | Shallow | Non-negotiables only |
| Thursday | Deep Work | Client delivery + system building (before 1:15pm) |
| Friday | Shallow | Non-negotiables only |
| Sat / Sun | Catch-up | Anything deferred from the week |

---

## Weekly Win Metric (check every Friday EOD)

- [ ] Outreach ran 5/7 days
- [ ] Closed or advanced at least 1 deal
- [ ] Published at least 2 pieces of content
- [ ] Delivered on all active client work

**4/4** = strong week. **3/4** = acceptable. **Below 3** = something broke - diagnose it, don't just push harder.
