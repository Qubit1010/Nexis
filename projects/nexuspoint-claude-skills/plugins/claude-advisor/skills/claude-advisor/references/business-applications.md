## How to use this when pitching AI work

- **Pitching clients:** lead with the *outcome* the case studies prove (hours saved, faster cycle time, fewer errors), not "we use AI." Map the prospect's function to the closest case study above.
- **The wedge:** these are AI-automation outcomes. Position AI automation as the premium offer; web/app work is the upsell, not the opener.
- **Believable ROI framing:** quote a real comparable (e.g. "a sales-ops platform automated 1.6M hours of admin and lifted win rates up to 40%" [s125]) rather than a generic "10x." Always cite that it's a published customer result, not a guarantee you're making.
