## Why this file exists

Every cold opener, every objection response, every discovery call references a NAMED PEER + a QUANTIFIED RESULT. Generic "we helped lots of clients" doesn't move the needle.

**Sourced finding (`references/research-synthesis.md`, Q1, Q4):** Specific named-peer + number combos outperform generic case studies by ~3-5x in cited reply/conversion rates.

This file is the queryable bank. The OpenAI generators (`linkedin-outreach`, `instagram-outreach`) pull from here when matching peer claims to prospects.

---

## How to use this bank

When you need a proof element in any DM, email, or call:

1. **Match by industry first** (closest fit beats best fit)
2. **Match by stack second** (if they're on Shopify, pull a Shopify case)
3. **Match by pain pattern third** (manual handoff, lead routing, recon, etc.)

If no close match exists → use the next-closest with a "similar team" framing.
