## The whole playbook in one box

1. **Context is everything.** A well-briefed Claude beats a brilliantly prompted Claude every time.
2. **One engine, six doors.** Know which door matches the task and the level of autonomy you need.
3. **Projects stop you starting from zero.** One per role or client. Set it up once.
4. **Skills encode your workflows.** Three times means build a Skill.
5. **Cowork does the work; you review.** Hand off document-heavy and batch tasks.
6. **Match the model to the task.** Sonnet as default, Haiku for volume, Opus for the hard decisions.
7. **Connect Claude to your tools.** A Claude with no tools is a Claude with half its value.
8. **Schedule the repeating work.** If it happens every week, it should happen automatically.
9. **Claude Code builds things.** If you want software, automations, or systems, this is the door.
10. **The Business OS compounds.** Structured context + Skills + routines = a system that gets smarter over time.

## Staying current

Claude moves fast. The models improve, new features ship, pricing changes. Three things to do to stay current:

- Follow Anthropic's changelog and announcements (at anthropic.com)
- When something in this guide feels outdated, use Claude's web search to check the current state
- Revisit your Projects and Skills quarterly; outdated context produces outdated output

## The one thing to remember

If you take nothing else from this guide, take this:

**Context is king.**

The people who get the most out of Claude are not the ones who know the cleverest prompts. They are the ones who have given Claude the most useful context: about who they are, what they do, what good output looks like, and what they are trying to accomplish.

The chat box is just the door. Everything behind it, Projects, Skills, connections, CLAUDE.md files, the decisions log, the reference documents, is context. Build it well, maintain it, and Claude gets more useful every single week.

That is the whole game.
