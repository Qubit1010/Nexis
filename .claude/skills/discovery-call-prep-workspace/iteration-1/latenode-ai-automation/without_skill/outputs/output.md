# Latenode — Call Prep

Latenode is a no-code/low-code workflow automation platform. Think Zapier but cheaper and with broader AI model access — they claim 1,200+ AI models and 5,500+ integrations under one subscription. They're positioning themselves as "10x cheaper than Zapier" at scale.

**What to know:**
- 10,000+ active teams, 10M+ workflows/month — real product with real users
- Pricing: Free to $299/month. Also offers white-label iPaaS for SaaS companies
- Strong ratings: 4.9 Capterra, 4.7 G2
- Founded and growing — technically sophisticated audience (they support full JavaScript in workflows)

**They replied to your cold email about AI workflow automation** — so they're at least curious. The question is what specifically they want. They already live in the automation space so the conversation probably isn't about convincing them automation matters. More likely they want something custom built — either an internal tool, a custom integration, or something their platform doesn't handle natively.

**Possible angles:**
- Custom AI system or pipeline built on top of their stack
- Internal tooling (admin dashboard, reporting, etc.)
- Website/marketing improvements
- White-label tool development for their own clients

**Things to ask:**
- What made them respond — is there a specific project they're working on?
- What does their current internal dev capacity look like?
- Is there a timeline or urgency?

**Note:** Don't pitch basic automation to an automation company. Come with specifics about complex builds — LangGraph, RAG systems, agentic AI pipelines.
