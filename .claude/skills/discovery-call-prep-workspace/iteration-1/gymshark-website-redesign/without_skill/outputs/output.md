# Gymshark — Call Prep

Gymshark is a global fitness apparel brand — one of the biggest names in gym clothing. Founded 2012, they do women's and men's activewear and accessories across 25+ international markets.

**What to know:**
- Very large company — multi-currency, physical retail locations, hundreds of SKUs
- Current tech stack: Next.js, Contentful CMS, Shopify backend, Dynamic Yield personalization, mParticle analytics. They're well-resourced and technically sophisticated.
- Community-driven brand, known for influencer marketing and their "Gymshark Central" blog

**They reached out after seeing your work and want to talk about website improvements.**

At their scale, "website improvements" probably means something specific — a campaign page, a performance fix, a new feature — not a full overhaul. Their internal team likely handles the core site. They may want a specialist for a scoped project.

**Potential scope:**
- Campaign microsites or landing pages for product launches
- Performance optimization on specific pages
- AI-powered product recommendation or personalization feature
- Content automation for their blog/editorial

**Things to ask:**
- What specific improvement are they thinking about?
- What does their internal dev team look like and what's typically outsourced?
- What's the timeline and budget range?
- Is this going through a vendor/procurement process?

**Watch out:** This could be a big client but also a misfit — enterprise brands have procurement processes, slow payment cycles, and high expectations. Make sure the scope is clear and the relationship is direct before investing too much in the conversation.
