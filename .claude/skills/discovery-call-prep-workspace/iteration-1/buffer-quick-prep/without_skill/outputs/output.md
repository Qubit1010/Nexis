# Buffer — Quick Notes

Buffer is a social media management tool — schedule posts, analyze performance, manage comments across platforms like Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, X, YouTube. Founded 2010, 73 employees, ~$24.6M ARR. Small team, distributed globally.

They have a free tier and paid plans. Known for radical transparency — they publish revenue and salaries publicly. Founder is Joel Gascoigne.

**You have a call in 15 minutes and don't know much about the context.** Start by asking what brought this on.

**Possible things they might want:**
- Custom AI tooling or automation (they're already building AI features for users)
- Web development — marketing site improvements or landing pages
- Some kind of integration or custom build their small team doesn't have bandwidth for

**Quick questions to ask:**
- What brought this call about — what problem are you trying to solve?
- What does your internal team focus on and what do you typically outsource?
- What's the timeline?

Buffer is values-driven and thoughtful. Don't be pushy. Ask good questions and let them lead.
