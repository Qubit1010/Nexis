## The 2026 Reels reality: a discovery engine, not a follower feed

Reels rank independently of follower count - they're an interest-based discovery engine. **55% of Reels views come from non-followers**, making Reels the best cold-acquisition tool on the platform (avg reach rate **30.81%**, 2x+ static/carousel) [s130, s135]. That means a small account can break out on content quality alone.

**Ranking signals, in order of weight** (per Mosseri) [s119, s184]:
1. **Watch time / completion** (highest)
2. **Sends per reach** - DM shares carry **3-5x** the weight of a like
3. **Likes per reach** (weakest)
Plus **Saves** = ~3x a like and extend evergreen shelf life.

Translation: optimize for completion + sends + saves. Likes are vanity.

## Length: completion beats duration

- **Sweet spot: 15-30 seconds** - highest completion + replay [s126].
- Up to 3 min is eligible for Explore, but only if retention holds. **A 15s reel at 80% completion crushes a 3-min reel at 20%** [s184]. Under 15s risks skips (no narrative arc).
- Aim for average watch time **>50%** of the reel's length.

---

## Win the first 3 seconds (this is the whole game)

**Up to 50% scroll past in 3 seconds** = 71% of total drop-off [s100, s184]. Benchmarks to hit:
- **3-second hold rate:** <50% kills distribution; **60%+ strong; 65-70%+ expands to non-followers; 75%+ exceptional** [s127].
- **Hook rate (3s plays / impressions): >25% good**, <15% degrades your CPM/distribution [s127].

How to hold them:
- **Motion in the very first frame** - if the brain registers a static image, they swipe. Open mid-action ("cold open"), not "Hey guys, today..." [s100].
- **Text in the center 72-75% safe zone** (avoid bottom 20% / top 10% UI) [s100].
- **High-impact words** ("you," "secret," "hack") boost early engagement +42%; visual pattern-interrupt + a direct question = +25% retention [s127].
- Assume muted viewing (60-85% watch on mute) - **kinetic captions are mandatory** (+25% retention) [s175].

## Benchmarks to track (ratio-based intent, not likes)

| Metric | Avg | Top 25% |
|--------|-----|---------|
| Avg watch time | 8.4s | 18s+ |
| Completion rate | 42% | 68% |
| Shares-to-reach | 0.8% | 2.5% (>3% = viral) |
| Saves-to-reach | 1.2% | 3.8% |

Source [s120]. If shares-to-reach hits 1-2%, the content is strong; above 3% it's spreading through DMs.

## Cadence & timing

- **3-5 Reels/week** is the sustainable sweet spot. Going from 1-2 to 3-5/week = ~12% more reach/post and 2x weekly follower growth (0.12% -> 0.26%) [s98].
- **Windows (local):** 7-9am, 11am-1pm, 7-10pm. Post **15-30 min before** a peak so it's indexed when users open the app [s98].
- **3-5 niche hashtags** (not 30 - that's a spam penalty); captions + on-screen text carry the semantic signal [s119].

## B2B + personal brand: how Reels actually convert

Reels are top-of-funnel awareness; they rarely convert directly. Build a funnel [s115, s132]:
- **Reels (TOFU)** = reach/awareness -> **Carousels (MOFU)** = saves/trust -> **Stories (BOFU)** = direct selling.
- **Personal profiles get 8x the engagement** of company pages - lead with the founder, not the NexusPoint page [s85].
- **Native-style beats polished.** Raw, phone-shot, ambient-lit talking-head footage outperforms cinematic commercials [s57].
- **DM-integrated hooks for lead capture:** "Comment SCRIPT" / "Comment HOOK" triggers an automated DM (Manychat/Inrō) that delivers the resource and captures a qualified lead - far better than chasing vanity views [s100]. Note: keep automation to DMs triggered by the user's own comment; cold DM blasting gets accounts banned (see instagram-outreach skill).

For NexusPoint: repurpose carousels/infographics into Reels (the **reel-creator** skill does this), drive saves with educational carousels, and use comment-to-DM to hand warm leads into the sales-playbook DM sequence.

## What NO LONGER works (kill list)

- **Reposting watermarked TikToks** - AI vision penalizes watermarks; 10+ reposts in 30 days = excluded from Explore. Original content gets +40-60% distribution [s119].
- **Hashtag stuffing** (30 tags) = spam penalty; use 3-5 niche [s119].
- **Engagement bait** ("Comment YES," "Like for Part 2") throttles reach [s119].
- **Slow intros** ("Hey guys, today...") waste the 3s window [s100].
- **Post and ghost** - the first-60-min "Golden Hour" matters; engage niche creators ~15 min before posting and reply to early comments [s184].
