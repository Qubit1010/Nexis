# Copper & Ground — Brand Brief

**Business:** Copper & Ground Specialty Coffee
**Location:** Brooklyn, NY (one cafe, no franchise plans)
**Founded:** 2019

## Services / Products
- Specialty espresso bar (single-origin rotating menu)
- Pour-over and batch brew
- Retail whole-bean bags (rotating single origins, sourced direct from farms)
- Monthly subscription coffee boxes
- Coffee education events (cuppings, brew method workshops, farm-to-cup talks)

## Target Audience
- Coffee enthusiasts in Brooklyn and surrounding areas, 24-38 years old
- People curious about specialty coffee but intimidated by it (accessible, not pretentious)
- Remote workers looking for a third place with reliable WiFi and good coffee
- Gift-buyers looking for high-quality subscription or single-bag options

## Brand Voice
Warm, curious, grounded. We take coffee seriously but we're not snobs. We want people to feel welcomed whether they're ordering their first pour-over or they've been going to origin trips for 10 years. We don't gatekeep. We explain things clearly without talking down to people.

## Colors
- Copper: #B87333
- Deep Roast Brown: #3B2314
- Cream: #F9F4EC
- Sage: #8A9A7B

## Typography
- Headings: Canela (editorial serif)
- Body: Aktiv Grotesk

## Key Differentiators
- Every bag on the menu has a one-page origin story — who grew it, how it was processed, what it tastes like and why
- Baristas can explain the flavor differences between any two coffees on the bar without being condescending
- No dark roasts — all coffees are light to medium to let the origin character come through
- Events are free or low-cost — treating education as community, not upsell

## What We Don't Say
- "the best coffee in Brooklyn" (unverifiable, sounds like a claim we can't back)
- "artisanal" (overused)
- Coffee jargon without explanation (we always explain terms like "anaerobic process" or "terroir" when we use them)
