# Apex Strength — Brand Brief

**Business:** Apex Strength Coaching
**Location:** Austin, Texas (in-person + online coaching)
**Founded:** 2021

## Services
- 1-on-1 personal training (in-person, Austin studio)
- Online programming (custom 12-week strength plans)
- Nutrition coaching (macro-based, no diet culture)
- Small group training (max 4 people per session)

## Target Audience
- Professionals aged 28-42 who have tried gyms before but can't stay consistent
- People who are frustrated with fad diets and generic workout programs
- Women who want to build strength without "bulking up"
- Men who have been training casually and want structured, measurable progress

## Brand Voice
Direct, no-nonsense, warm but not soft. We don't use motivational poster language. We talk to adults who have tried and failed before and are skeptical of fitness promises. Evidence-backed. Practical. We avoid the phrase "transform your body" and anything that implies shame about current fitness levels.

## Typography
- Headings: Neue Haas Grotesk Display
- Body: Inter

## Key Differentiators
- No 6-week challenges or crash programs — minimum commitment is 12 weeks
- Coaches have personal training certifications AND experience in powerlifting/strength sport
- Nutrition coaching is not a meal plan — it's education so clients stop needing a coach
- Small group max 4: clients know each other by name, coach knows every client's history

## What We Don't Say
- "transform your body"
- "melt fat"
- "get the body you've always wanted"
- Anything implying clients should be ashamed of where they are now
