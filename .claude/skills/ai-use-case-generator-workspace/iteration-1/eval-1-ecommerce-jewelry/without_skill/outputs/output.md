**AI Use Cases: Handmade Jewelry E-commerce Brand**

**Prospect Profile:** E-commerce brand, handmade jewelry, 8 employees, Shopify store + spreadsheet inventory, 4+ hours/day on customer support emails, high return volume processed manually.

---

**Use Case 1: AI Customer Support Agent (Tier-1 Email Automation)**

An AI agent trained on your product catalog, policies, and FAQ handles incoming customer emails automatically. It drafts or sends responses for the top 80% of repeatable queries: order status, shipping timelines, return initiation, product care instructions, size questions, and customization inquiries. Anything it can't resolve with confidence gets escalated to a human with a pre-drafted reply ready to review.

ROI framing:
- 4+ hours/day on support = $300+/week in labor cost (~$15,000+/year equivalent)
- AI handles 70-80% of tickets without human touch
- Frees up 3+ hours/day across the team
- Payback period: under 60 days
- Bonus: 24/7 response capability improves customer satisfaction and repeat purchase rate

---

**Use Case 2: Unified Inventory Sync + Low-Stock Alert System**

Eliminates the Shopify-spreadsheet split. An automation layer keeps inventory in sync in real time, and an AI alert system flags low-stock items, predicts restock timing based on sales velocity, and surfaces stockout risks before they happen.

ROI framing:
- Manual dual-tracking causes oversells -- especially painful for handmade items with limited production capacity
- Saves 30-60 minutes/day in manual reconciliation
- A single prevented oversell avoids refund cost + customer acquisition cost of replacing that buyer
- At $75 AOV and even 1 prevented oversell/week: ~$3,900/year recovered

---

**Use Case 3: Automated Returns Processing + Deflection Workflow**

Two-part system. First, an AI deflection layer identifies return requests early and attempts resolution before the return is initiated -- offering exchanges, store credit, or care instructions. Second, for returns that proceed, the workflow auto-generates return labels, updates Shopify inventory, triggers the refund, and logs return reasons for trend analysis.

ROI framing:
- Handmade jewelry: higher cost per unit, harder to resell if damaged
- 10 returns/month at $75 AOV = $750/month flowing out + 2-4 hours of manual processing
- 20-30% deflection rate (converting to exchanges/credit) recovers $150-$225/month in revenue
- Full automation eliminates manual processing labor
- Combined: estimated 5-8 hours/day freed and $18,000-$25,000+/year in direct + indirect savings
