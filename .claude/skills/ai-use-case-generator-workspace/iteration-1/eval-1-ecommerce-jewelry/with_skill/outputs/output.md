**AI Use Cases for Handmade Jewelry E-commerce Brand (8 employees)**

---

**Use Case 1: AI Customer Support Inbox Automation**
**Dimension:** Operational Efficiency
**Problem:** The team is spending 4+ hours a day answering customer support emails — a mix of order status questions, shipping inquiries, sizing/product questions, and return requests. At 8 employees, that's a significant chunk of productive capacity eaten by inbox triage every single day.
**Solution:** An AI support agent connected to their email inbox (or a simple chat widget) that automatically handles the top 40-60% of incoming tickets — order status lookups via Shopify API, shipping updates, return initiation, and FAQ responses — routing only complex or high-value conversations to a human.
**ROI:** Deflects 40-60% of support tickets automatically, saving 15-20 hours/week across the team. At a conservative $15-20/hour equivalent, that's $900-$1,600/month in reclaimed staff time without hiring anyone new.
**Hook:** "Most e-commerce brands we work with reclaim 15-20 hours a week just by automating the top half of their support inbox — order status, shipping updates, and return requests — without touching the conversations that actually need a human."

---

**Use Case 2: Inventory Sync and Low-Stock Alert System**
**Dimension:** Revenue / Growth
**Problem:** Manually reconciling inventory between Shopify and a spreadsheet is time-consuming and error-prone. Stockouts on best-selling SKUs mean lost sales that never get recovered — customers who hit an "out of stock" page rarely come back. For handmade jewelry with longer production lead times, catching low stock early is especially critical.
**Solution:** An automation that syncs Shopify inventory into a single source of truth (eliminating the manual spreadsheet entirely), monitors stock levels per SKU, and sends proactive alerts when any product drops below a set threshold — giving the team time to restock before selling out. A weekly report surfaces top-selling SKUs, slow movers, and revenue-at-risk items automatically.
**ROI:** Eliminates 3-5 hours/week of manual inventory tracking. More importantly, reduces stockouts by 20-30% — for a brand doing $15K+/month, recovering even one stockout event per month can add $500-$2,000 in sales that would otherwise be lost permanently.
**Hook:** "Handmade brands are especially exposed to stockouts because you can't scale production overnight — an automated inventory alert system means you're restocking before you run out, not after you've already missed the sale."

---

**Use Case 3: Automated Returns Processing with Post-Return Recovery**
**Dimension:** Customer Experience
**Problem:** Processing returns manually — reading the email, verifying the order, issuing the label, updating inventory, processing the refund — takes significant time per return. With a high return volume, this compounds fast. Most brands stop at the refund and move on, missing the window to convert a return into an exchange or retain the customer relationship.
**Solution:** An automated returns flow where the customer submits a return request via a simple form or email trigger, the system checks the order against Shopify automatically, generates a return label, triggers the refund or store credit based on configurable rules, and sends a personalized follow-up — either offering an exchange or a discount on their next order. Standard cases resolve same-day with zero human involvement.
**ROI:** Reduces return resolution time from 3-5 days to same-day for standard cases, saving 5-8 hours/week in manual handling. The post-return recovery email sequence typically converts 8-15% of returners into exchanges or repeat purchases — turning a cost center into a partial revenue recovery channel.
**Hook:** "Right now every return your team processes manually is costing you twice — once to handle the logistics, and again because you're not following up to keep the customer. An automated returns flow fixes both in a single build."

**Quick pitch summary**
- AI support inbox agent that deflects 40-60% of emails automatically, saving 15-20 hours/week
- Shopify inventory sync that kills the spreadsheet and sends low-stock alerts before you run out
- Automated returns flow that resolves standard cases same-day and converts 8-15% of returners into repeat buyers
