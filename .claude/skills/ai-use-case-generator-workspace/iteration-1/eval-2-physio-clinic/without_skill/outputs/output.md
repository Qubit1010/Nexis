# AI Use Cases: Physiotherapy Clinic (3 Locations)

**Business profile:** Multi-location physio clinic -- manual scheduling, high staff time on follow-up calls, slow insurance claim processing

---

## Use Case 1: AI Appointment Scheduling & Rescheduling

**What it does:**
An AI assistant (voice or web chat) handles all inbound bookings, rescheduling, and cancellations across all 3 locations -- 24/7, no staff involvement. Integrates with existing scheduling software. Sends automated SMS/email confirmations and reminders.

**The problem it solves:**
Front desk staff spend 2-3 hours/day on scheduling calls. After-hours calls go unanswered -- missed calls become missed bookings.

**ROI framing:**
- If AI captures just 5 missed/after-hours bookings per week across locations at $80-150/session, that's $400-750/week recovered -- $20,000-39,000/year from appointments that would otherwise be lost
- Front desk redirects 10+ hours/week to higher-value in-clinic work
- No additional headcount needed to scale booking volume

---

## Use Case 2: Automated Patient Follow-Up & Recall System

**What it does:**
AI sends personalized post-session messages -- checking recovery, reinforcing home exercises, and prompting rebooking when the next session is due. Flags patients who go cold so staff know exactly who to prioritize.

**The problem it solves:**
Staff spend hours manually calling lapsed patients. Drop-off after initial sessions is 30-50% industry-wide. Inconsistent follow-up means lost patients and lost revenue.

**ROI framing:**
- Average patient LTV: 6-10 sessions at $80-150 = $480-1,500 per patient
- Recovering 10 lapsed patients/month across 3 locations = $4,800-15,000/month in retained revenue
- Staff follow-up time drops 60-70% -- freeing 5-8 hours/week per location
- Better care continuity is also a differentiator patients notice

---

## Use Case 3: AI-Assisted Insurance Claim Processing

**What it does:**
AI extracts session data and patient info, auto-fills claim forms, flags errors and missing fields before submission, and tracks claim status. Reduces the manual work and error rate driving your rejection and delay problem.

**The problem it solves:**
Every rejected claim = rework, delayed payment, and staff hours chasing. At 3 locations processing 200+ claims/month, even a 5-10% rejection rate is a constant drain on cash flow and admin time.

**ROI framing:**
- Industry average rejection rate 5-10%; each rejection costs $25-50 in rework plus delayed cash flow
- AI cuts rejections 50-70% by catching errors pre-submission -- $125-700/month saved on rework alone, plus faster payment cycles
- Staff time on claims drops 40-60% -- 1-2 hours/day per location recovered
- Cleaner submissions = faster approvals = better cash flow across all 3 locations

---

**Closing pitch line:** This isn't software. It's a system that makes your clinic run like it has twice the staff -- without adding headcount.
