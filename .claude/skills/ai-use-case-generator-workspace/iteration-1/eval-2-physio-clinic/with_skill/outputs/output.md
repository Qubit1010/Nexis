**AI Use Cases for Physiotherapy Clinic (3 Locations)**

---

**Use Case 1: Automated Appointment Scheduling + No-Show Recovery**
**Dimension:** Operational Efficiency
**Problem:** Staff at all 3 locations are manually handling appointment bookings, confirmations, and rescheduling over the phone. With a typical no-show rate of 15-25% in allied health, they're losing revenue on every empty slot that could have been filled from the waitlist.
**Solution:** An AI-powered scheduling assistant handles booking confirmations, sends automated SMS/email reminders 48 and 24 hours before appointments, and automatically texts the waitlist to fill last-minute cancellations — all without front-desk involvement.
**ROI:** Reduces no-show rate from ~20% to 5-10%, which for a 3-location clinic typically recovers $80K-$120K/year in unbilled slots. Front-desk staff reclaim 2-3 hours/day per location previously spent on reminder calls and manual rescheduling.
**Hook:** "A 3-location physio clinic running at a 20% no-show rate is leaving $80K-$120K on the table every year — we've seen clinics cut that in half with automated reminders and waitlist filling, without touching their existing booking system."

---

**Use Case 2: Insurance Pre-Authorization + Claim Status Automation**
**Dimension:** Revenue / Growth
**Problem:** Insurance claim processing is a bottleneck — staff spend hours per day on hold with insurers, manually tracking claim statuses, and chasing pre-authorizations. Slow turnaround delays patient starts and creates cash flow gaps between service delivery and reimbursement.
**Solution:** An AI workflow automatically submits pre-authorization requests, monitors claim status via insurer portals, flags exceptions that need human review, and sends staff a daily summary of pending and approved claims — reducing the manual chase to exception handling only.
**ROI:** Cuts pre-authorization turnaround from 3-5 days to same-day for standard procedures. Across 3 locations, this typically saves 8-15 hours/week of admin time and accelerates cash collection by 10-20 days on average.
**Hook:** "Most multi-location clinics have 1-2 admin staff spending half their day on insurance follow-ups — we can get that down to exception handling only, which usually cuts authorization turnaround from days to hours."

---

**Use Case 3: Automated Post-Visit Patient Follow-Up**
**Dimension:** Customer Experience
**Problem:** Physio patients need consistent follow-up between sessions to stay on their treatment plan, but staff don't have time to call every patient after every visit. Patients who feel disconnected between appointments are more likely to drop off before completing their treatment.
**Solution:** An AI system sends personalized post-visit messages (via SMS or WhatsApp) after each appointment: a check-in on how they're feeling, a reminder of their home exercises, and a prompt to rebook. Patients who don't rebook within 48 hours get a gentle automated nudge.
**ROI:** Saves 2-3 hours/day of post-visit phone follow-up across the clinic network. In similar allied health settings, automated follow-up typically improves rebooking rates by 15-25%, directly lifting per-patient revenue.
**Hook:** "Patients who don't hear from you after a session are the ones who quietly stop coming back — automated post-visit check-ins typically lift rebooking rates by 15-25% without adding any staff workload."

---

**Quick pitch summary**
- Automated scheduling reminders and waitlist filling recover $80K-$120K/year lost to no-shows across 3 locations
- AI-driven insurance pre-authorization cuts 8-15 hours/week of admin follow-up and accelerates cash collection by 10-20 days
- Personalized post-visit follow-up automation increases patient rebooking rates by 15-25% without adding staff workload
