## Why do prospects stop replying in DMs?

It is almost never because they hate you. It is one of five reasons.

### 1. They got distracted mid-conversation

Instagram is built to interrupt. A notification, a Reel, a story. They meant to reply. They did not. The conversation falls below the fold and dies.

### 2. They felt pressure on the next step

You asked them to book a call. They are not ready. Most coaches read silence as a no. It is usually a "not yet." See how to book calls from DMs without being pushy.

### 3. They saw the price and froze

You dropped a hint at the investment. They went quiet. They are not rejecting you. They are checking their bank account, their partner, their calendar. Handle this with pricing objection sequences.

### 4. They were not qualified to begin with

Some leads were never going to buy. Better lead qualification upstream cuts ghost rate by half because you stop chasing tire-kickers.

### 5. They are slow-burn buyers

The average coaching prospect needs 7 to 12 touches before booking. If you stop at touch 2, you ghost them. They did not ghost you.

## How does AI re-engage ghosted DM prospects?

A trained AI setter does three things humans rarely do consistently.

### It references the actual conversation

Generic "hey, still around?" gets ignored. AI pulls the specific thread. "Hey Sarah, you mentioned you wanted to lose 15 pounds before your sister's wedding in October. Still on track?" That gets answered.

### It picks the right time

Most coaches re-engage at random. AI fires at the window the prospect is actually on Instagram, based on their reply patterns. Tuesday at 8:47 PM beats Sunday at 11 AM for most coaching audiences.

### It uses loss-aversion in the breakup

The final touch is not "any update?" It is "Hey, I am closing my June calendar tomorrow. If now is not the right time, totally understand. I will assume you are good and stop reaching out." This pulls a 28% reply rate. Most are positive.

## A 5-touch re-engagement sequence that works

Run this on every ghosted lead from the last 90 days.

1. Day 2: Reference the exact pain they shared. No question. Just an observation. "Been thinking about what you said about your energy crashes at 3 PM."
2. Day 5: Share a relevant story or insight. Zero ask. "Had a client in your exact spot last month. She is now waking up before her alarm."
3. Day 10: Soft re-open. "Curious where you landed on the energy thing?"
4. Day 17: Pattern interrupt. Send a voice note, a Loom, or a meme that fits the conversation.
5. Day 23: Loss-aversion breakup. "Closing my doors for new clients on Friday. If I do not hear back, I will assume the timing is off."

Each touch is generated dynamically based on what they said earlier in the thread. Generic templates do not work here. See why dynamic scripts beat static templates.

## FAQ

### How long should I wait before re-engaging a ghosted prospect?

48 hours minimum, 5 days maximum. Sooner feels needy. Later feels random. Day 2 is the sweet spot for the first touch.

### Does Instagram penalize re-engagement DMs?

Not when they are conversational and one-to-one. Mass-blast bulk DMs trigger Meta's spam filters. Conversational AI replies that follow the Meta Messenger rules do not.

### How many times can I follow up before it is harassment?

Five to seven touches over 21 to 30 days is the industry norm. Beyond that, you are pushing. Most ghost-recovery happens between touch 3 and touch 5.

### What is the highest-reply re-engagement message?

The loss-aversion breakup. "I am closing my calendar for new clients on Friday. If now is not the right time, no worries. I will stop reaching out." 28% reply rate across 42,000+ conversations.
