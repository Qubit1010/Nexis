# Reel Strategy — NexusPoint

Reels are the only format Instagram shows to non-followers.
One good Reel does what 30 carousels can't — puts you in front of a cold SaaS founder who's never heard of NexusPoint.

---

## The 3 Reel Jobs

Know which job each Reel is doing before you make it.

| Job | Goal | Who sees it |
|-----|------|-------------|
| **Reach** | Get in front of new people | Non-followers via Explore and Reels tab |
| **Trust** | Convert viewers to followers | Cold viewers who watch to the end |
| **Convert** | Turn followers into DMs | Existing followers who see it in feed |

Pick one job per Reel. Most Reels try to do all three and do none well.

---

## The 5 Reel Formats

### Format 1 — Workflow Reveal (easiest, highest reach)

**Job:** Reach + Trust | **Effort:** Low — no face, just screen

Show a live automation running or being built. Hook is the result, not the process.

**Script:**
```
[0-2 sec]  Text overlay: "This runs 24/7. Zero humans involved."
[2-20 sec] Screen recording — workflow executing or being built
           Text callouts on each step: "Scrapes here" / "Enriches here" / "Sends here"
[20-35 sec] Show the output — spreadsheet filling, email sending, Slack ping
[35-45 sec] Text overlay: "Built in 3 days. DM me if you want this for your team."
```

**Why it works:** "Watching something work automatically" is visceral. ICP stops because they immediately think "we do that manually."

**Specific ideas:**
- LinkedIn outreach pipeline running end to end
- Lead scoring system ingesting raw data → scored list output
- Client onboarding triggered automatically from a form
- Content engine generating and logging a post
- CRM updating itself from an email thread

---

### Format 2 — 30-Second Opinion (medium effort, builds personal brand)

**Job:** Trust + Convert | **Effort:** Medium — face on camera, one take

One contrarian or specific opinion. Direct to camera. No slides, no graphics.

**Script:**
```
[0-3 sec]  Hook — the opinion, stated boldly
[3-25 sec] The argument — 2-3 supporting points, one sentence each
[25-35 sec] The flip — what to do instead, or why this matters
[35-45 sec] (optional) Soft CTA — "follow if you build ops teams"
```

**Why it works:** Builds Aleem the person, not NexusPoint the agency. People follow people.

**Specific ideas:**
- "Most automation agencies will sell you a Zapier workflow and call it AI. Here's the difference."
- "I turned down a $4K project last week. Here's why it was the right call."
- "Everyone's building AI chatbots. Nobody's building the thing after the chatbot."
- "The most expensive hire you'll make is the person doing a job a system should be doing."
- "I'm a 25-year-old student in Pakistan building systems for companies in the US and UK. Here's how."
- "You don't need a bigger ops team. You need a smaller ops surface."
- "The problem with most no-code automations: they break the moment the ICP changes."

---

### Format 3 — Before/After (medium effort, highest conversion)

**Job:** Convert — turns viewers into DM senders | **Effort:** Medium

Show the manual process, then the automated version. Contrast does the selling.

**Script:**
```
[0-3 sec]  Text: "Before NexusPoint" or "What their ops looked like on day 1"
[3-15 sec] The before — spreadsheet, manual Slack, someone copy-pasting
[15-18 sec] Transition — cut or text: "After"
[18-35 sec] The after — automation running, output appearing, nobody touching it
[35-45 sec] The number: "Saved 15 hrs/week" + CTA
```

**Why it works:** When ICP sees their own workflow in the "before," they DM.

**Specific ideas:**
- Manual lead follow-up (person in Sheets) → pipeline handling it automatically
- Client onboarding checklist → triggered flow with zero human steps
- CRM updating manually → AI reading email and updating it
- Reporting pulled manually → dashboard auto-generating the report

---

### Format 4 — Build Timelapse (low effort, high shareability)

**Job:** Reach + Trust | **Effort:** Low — record while you work, speed it up

Record building something in n8n, Claude Code, or Python. Speed up to 30-45 sec. Add text callouts.

**Script:**
```
[0-2 sec]  Text: "Built this in 2 hours. Here's the timelapse."
[2-40 sec] Sped-up screen recording at 10-15x speed
           Key moments highlighted with text callouts
[40-45 sec] Text: "What it does: [one line]" + "DM 'BUILD' if you need one"
```

**Why it works:** Watching someone work at 10x speed is addictive. Signals competence without claiming it.

---

### Format 5 — Myth Bust (medium effort, highest share rate)

**Job:** Reach — most likely to be shared | **Effort:** Medium

Challenge a common belief your ICP holds that's costing them time or money.

**Script:**
```
[0-3 sec]  State the myth: "Everyone says [X]"
[3-20 sec] Why it's wrong — specific, not vague
[20-35 sec] What's actually true — the better mental model
[35-45 sec] The implication for their business
```

**Specific ideas:**
- "Myth: more automation tools = more efficiency" (reality: tool sprawl creates new manual work)
- "Myth: you need a big team to scale ops" (reality: fewer processes, not more people)
- "Myth: AI will replace your team" (reality: AI will replace teams that don't use it)
- "Myth: Zapier is automation" (reality: Zapier is plumbing, not automation)
- "Myth: hire an ops manager to fix ops chaos" (reality: the chaos is the process, not the person)

## The Hook — The Only Thing That Actually Matters

Instagram decides in 1.5 seconds whether to keep showing your Reel. That decision is based entirely on whether viewers watch past the hook.

**Hook formulas:**
```
"[Specific result] in [specific time]. Here's exactly how."
→ "Cut a client's manual ops by 70% in 3 weeks. Here's exactly how."

"Nobody talks about [specific thing]. So I will."
→ "Nobody talks about the ops wall every company hits at 25 employees. So I will."

"[Common thing] is [wrong/broken/a lie]. Here's why."
→ "Your automation stack is costing you more time than it saves. Here's why."

"I [did unusual thing]. Here's what I learned."
→ "I turned down a $5K project last week. Here's what I learned."

"Stop [doing X]. Do [Y] instead."
→ "Stop hiring for ops roles. Automate the role first."

"[Number] [ICP] still do [thing] manually. Don't be one of them."
→ "80% of SaaS founders still do client onboarding manually. Don't be one of them."
```

**Hook delivery rules:**
- First word on screen or spoken within 0.5 seconds — no slow fade-ins
- Never start with your name, your company, or "hey guys"
- Hook should create a question only watching resolves
- If it works as a tweet standalone — it'll work as a Reel hook

## Audio Strategy

| Option | Best for | Notes |
|--------|---------|-------|
| Original audio (talking head) | Opinion + myth bust Reels | Voice becomes searchable, others can use your audio |
| Trending audio (CapCut library) | BTS + timelapse content | Match energy to content — not on opinion pieces |
| No audio + captions | Screen recording content | Background music at 20% volume, royalty-free from CapCut |

Never use copyrighted music — Instagram mutes it and the Reel dies.

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Dimensions | 1080 x 1920 (9:16 vertical) |
| Length | 30-45 seconds sweet spot |
| File format | MP4 |
| Captions | Always on — 85% watched muted |
| Cover image | Custom still, not a random frame |
| Caption length | 80-120 words max |

## Posting and Timing

**Frequency:** 1 Reel/week minimum. 2/week if quality holds.

**Best times (PKT):**
- Tuesday or Wednesday 7-9pm — US East Coast midday, UK afternoon
- Friday 6-8pm — US morning, UK afternoon

Avoid Monday — people are heads-down.

**The 60-minute engagement window:**
Reply to every comment in the first 60 minutes after posting. Instagram reads early engagement velocity as a quality signal. More early engagement = more distribution.

## What to Measure (check every Friday)

| Metric | What it tells you | Action |
|--------|------------------|--------|
| Reach (non-followers) | Distribution reach | High = replicate format and hook |
| Watch time % | Hook + content quality | Under 30% = rewrite the hook |
| Saves | Content value | High = make more of this type |
| Profile visits | ICP attraction | High = hook is working on right audience |
| Follows from Reel | Net growth driver | Track which Reels drove the most follows |

**The number that matters most: profile visits from Reels.**
- High profile visits + low follows = profile not converting (fix the profile)
- Low profile visits = hook not attracting the right people (fix the hook)
