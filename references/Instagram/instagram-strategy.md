## The 3 Content Pillars

Everything posted should fit one of these. If it doesn't, don't post it.

### Pillar 1 — Proof (40%)
Show results, not capabilities. ICP cares about what's been done, not what's possible.
- "We cut this team's manual onboarding by 70%. Here's the exact system." → carousel walking through the build
- Client win screenshots (anonymized if needed) with context
- Before/after ops workflow comparisons

### Pillar 2 — Education (40%)
Teach the ICP one thing per post in their language — ops pain, not AI trends.
- "5 processes every 30-person SaaS team should automate before hiring"
- "Why your ops team is slow (it's not a people problem)"
- "What happens to your manual processes when you scale past 25 employees"

### Pillar 3 — Behind the Scenes (20%)
Show the build process. Makes NexusPoint real vs. another faceless agency.
- Screen recordings of building in n8n or Claude Code
- "Here's the workflow I built this morning for a client"
- Workspace, process, tools

## Format Breakdown

| Format | Purpose | Frequency |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Carousels (5-8 slides) | Education + saves + reach | 3x/week |
| Reels (30-60 sec) | New audience reach | 1x/week |
| Stories | Nurture existing followers + DM triggers | Daily |

- **Reels** = reach engine, shown to non-followers
- **Carousels** = get saved and shared, compound over time
- **Stories** = convert existing followers into conversations

## Caption Formula

```
Line 1: Bold hook — same energy as the first slide
[blank line]
Lines 2-4: The insight, specific and concrete
[blank line]
Line 5: The pull — "This is what we built for a client last week"
[blank line]
CTA: "Save this if your ops team is dealing with the same thing"
     or "DM me 'AUDIT' if you want to see what's worth automating"
```

Max 150 words. Every line earns the next.

## Story Poll Strategy (highest ROI move)

Once a week, post a poll in Stories:

> "How many hours does your team spend on manual data entry or follow-ups per week?"
> - Under 5 hours
> - 5-15 hours
> - Send help (15+)

Anyone who votes "Send help" gets a DM within 24 hours:
> "It sounds like your team is carrying a lot of manual work — what's the process that's eating the most time right now?"

Warm, permission-based conversation starter. No cold pitching needed.

## What to Stop Doing

- Meme posts — confuses ICP, breaks professional positioning
- Broad AI trend content ("Is AI taking over?" style) — attracts AI enthusiasts, not buyers
- Posting without a CTA — every post needs one action for the reader to take
