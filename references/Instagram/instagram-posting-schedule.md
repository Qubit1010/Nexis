## Core Rule: 3-5 Reels/Week, Not Per Day

Instagram's Reels algorithm is a discovery engine — **55% of Reels views come from non-followers** [s130, s135]. The algo ranks by content quality signals, not volume. Flooding the feed dilutes those signals.

Going from 1-2 to 3-5 Reels/week = **+12% reach per post and 2x weekly follower growth** (0.12% -> 0.26%) [s98]. Beyond 5/week, gains plateau and quality typically drops.

## What the Algorithm Actually Ranks (in order)

1. **Watch time / completion** - highest weight. A 15s reel at 80% completion beats a 3-min reel at 20% [s184].
2. **Sends per reach** - DM shares carry 3-5x the weight of a like [s119, s184].
3. **Saves** - ~3x a like; extends evergreen shelf life.
4. **Likes** - weakest signal. Do not optimize for these.

## The Golden 3 Seconds (This is the Whole Game)

Up to **50% scroll past in 3 seconds** = 71% of total drop-off [s100, s184].

How to hold them:
- **Open mid-action (cold open)** - never "Hey guys, today I want to talk about..."
- **Motion in the very first frame** - a static opening frame = instant swipe
- **60-85% of Reels are watched on mute** - kinetic captions are mandatory (+25% retention) [s175]
- Text in the **center 72-75% safe zone** (avoid bottom 20% and top 10% - covered by UI)
- High-impact words ("you," "secret," "hack") boost early engagement +42% [s127]

---

## Ideal Reel Length

- **Sweet spot: 15-30 seconds** - highest completion and replay rate [s126]
- Up to 3 min is eligible for Explore, but only if retention holds
- Under 15s risks skips (no narrative arc)
- Aim for average watch time >50% of the reel's total length

## Weekly Content Funnel

Reels alone don't convert. Run three formats as a funnel [s115, s132]:

| Format | Role | Key Signal |
|--------|------|------------|
| Reels (15-30s) | Top-of-funnel awareness, cold reach | Watch time, sends |
| Carousels | Mid-funnel trust, education | Saves, swipes |
| Stories | Bottom-of-funnel selling, warm audience | Replies, link taps |

**Clean weekly rhythm:**
- 3-4 Reels (hook-driven, educational or behind-the-build)
- 1-2 Carousels (save-worthy: frameworks, checklists, step-by-steps)
- Daily Stories (warm audience touchpoint — low effort, high retention)

**Personal profile vs. company page:** personal = 8x engagement [s85]. Lead with Aleem's face/voice, not the NexusPoint page.

**Native-style beats polished:** Raw, phone-shot, talking-head footage outperforms cinematic commercial-style content [s57].

## Lead Capture: Comment-to-DM (for NexusPoint)

Don't drive to a link in bio. Use comment-to-DM automation (Manychat / Inro):
- Post ends with "Comment SCRIPT and I'll send it"
- User comments -> they get the resource via DM automatically
- You capture a qualified warm lead
- Hand them into the sales-playbook DM sequence

This converts far better than chasing views with no capture mechanism [s100].

## Hashtags

- **3-5 niche hashtags only** (not 30 - that's a spam penalty) [s119]
- Caption semantics and on-screen text carry the algorithmic signal more than hashtags

---

## What Kills Reach

- **Reposting watermarked TikToks** - AI vision detects it; 10+ reposts in 30 days = excluded from Explore. Original content gets +40-60% distribution [s119].
- **30 hashtags** - spam penalty. Use 3-5 niche only.
- **Engagement bait** ("Comment YES", "Like for Part 2") - throttles reach.
- **Slow intros** ("Hey guys...") - wastes the 3-second window.
- **Post and ghost** - engage niche creators 15 min before posting, then reply to early comments in the first 60 min.

---

## Priority Order for Optimization

1. Win the first 3 seconds (cold open, motion, kinetic captions)
2. Optimize for completion and saves, not likes
3. Post 15-30 min before the peak window
4. Engage before posting + reply to early comments
5. Run the Reels -> Carousels -> Stories funnel
6. Add comment-to-DM capture on your best-performing Reels
