## FAQ

**Headline:** Frequently Asked Questions

**Sub-CTA:** Still Have Questions? — Ask A Question

**Q: How is this different from hiring a freelancer or in-house team?**
Freelancers sell time. In-house teams add fixed costs. We build systems that remove manual work, increase conversions, and scale with your business—without long-term payroll or micromanagement.

**Q: What kind of results should I expect?**
Results depend on your business model, traffic, and existing systems but most clients see: Faster operations through automation Higher conversion rates from optimized websites Lower acquisition costs with smarter marketing systems Our focus is always on measurable outcomes, not vanity features.

**Q: Do you work with small businesses or only enterprises?**
Both. If you're early-stage, we help you build a strong foundation. If you're scaling, we design advanced AI and automation systems to remove bottlenecks and increase leverage.

**Q: What tools and technologies do you use?**
We choose tools based on what actually works for your business, including: Webflow, WordPress, React, Next.js / n8n, Zapier, custom APIs / OpenAI, LangChain, vector databases / Google Ads, Meta Ads, analytics & CRMs. No vendor lock-in. No unnecessary tech.

**Q: How long does a typical project take?**
Most projects take 2–6 weeks, depending on complexity. Larger automation or AI systems may take longer but we deliver in phases, so you start seeing value early.

**Q: Will I be able to manage everything after delivery?**
Yes. We document workflows, provide handover training, and build systems that your team can run without us unless you want ongoing optimization.

**Q: Do you offer ongoing support or retainers?**
Yes. Many clients stay on a monthly optimization retainer for: Continuous improvements / New automations / Performance tracking / Marketing and conversion optimization. This keeps your systems improving as your business grows.

**Q: What if I don't know exactly what I need?**
That's normal. We start with a strategy call or automation audit to identify where the biggest gains are then build only what moves the needle.
